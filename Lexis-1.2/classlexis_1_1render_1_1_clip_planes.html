lexis::render::ClipPlanes::ClipPlanes ( )

Adds 6 othogonal planes in normalized space (+x,-x,+y,-y,+z,-z).

Convex region is defined as an AABB (-0.5,-0.5,-0.5) to (0.5, 0.5, 0.5).
